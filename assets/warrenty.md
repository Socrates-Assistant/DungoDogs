Dead Dingo Dongas – Voluntary Warranty against Defects
1. Our Consumer Guarantee
Our goods come with guarantees that cannot be excluded under the Australian Consumer Law. You are entitled to a replacement or refund for a major failure and compensation for any other reasonably foreseeable loss or damage. You are also entitled to have the goods repaired or replaced if the goods fail to be of acceptable quality and the failure does not amount to a major failure.
2. Warranty Period and Coverage
In addition to your rights under the Australian Consumer Law, Dead Dingo Dongas offers a voluntary 5-year standard structural warranty on our garden sheds from the date of purchase.
You can extend this coverage to a 20-year extended structural warranty by registering your proof of purchase online at www.deaddingodongas.com.au within 30 days of purchase.
Both the standard and extended warranties cover defects in materials and manufacturing workmanship under normal domestic use.
3. Warranty Exclusions
This warranty does not cover damage caused by:
• Incorrect DIY assembly or installation not conforming to our instructions.
• Inadequate or non-level foundations, footings, or concrete slabs.
• Severe weather events, accidents, floods, fire, or acts of God.
• Modification, alteration, or repairs made by unauthorised persons.
• Normal wear and tear, rust, or corrosion caused by coastal salt spray or chemical exposure.
• Use of the shed for commercial or non-intended purposes.
