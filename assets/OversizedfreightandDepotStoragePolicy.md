Oversized Freight & Depot Storage Policy

Due to the custom manufacturing process, heavy weight (1,100kg), and oversize dimensions (5,900mm) of our kits, all shipments are delivered to regional commercial transport depots. The customer is entirely responsible for collecting the goods within **14 business days** of depot arrival unless otherwise agreed in writing.

If the goods are not collected within this window, Dead Dingo Dongas reserves the right to redirect the physical inventory to the next available customer to mitigate ongoing freight storage fees. The customer's order will be rescheduled into our next production cycle. Any storage fees incurred from the freight provider prior to redirection will be billed directly to the customer or deducted from their account balance.
