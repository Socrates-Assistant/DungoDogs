Shipping & Delivery Details
1. Ex-Sydney or Brisbane Facility Pricing
All prices listed on our website are ex-factory from our Sydney facility, unless specifically stated otherwise. This means the base cost of the shed covers manufacturing only and does not include freight or transport to your location unless explicitly noted on the product or invoice.
2. Sydney/ Bris Metro Delivery Options
Due to the large size(up to 5.8m long), weight around 1120kg, and heavy-duty packaging of our garden shed kits, Hiab delivery will be required and relies on site access. Prices quoted will be based on proper access availability.
3 .Regional
• Depot-to-Depot Delivery: We ship your kit to the nearest commercial freight depot to your location for customer collection.
• Local Delivery Options: You can check with local transport operators or couriers to arrange delivery from the depot or facility directly to your site.
•  
3. Critical Pickup Requirements
If you are collecting your kit from our Sydney or Brisbane facility or a freight depot, you must arrive with an appropriate vehicle.
• All persons must agree to comply with WHS laws upon entering any transport facility
• A flat-bed truck or a car trailer is strictly required.
• Standard utes, vans, or box trailers are not suitable, as some structural components reach up to 5.8 metres in length. There are 2 bundles that total around 1120kg
• Ensure your vehicle or trailer is legally rated and equipped to secure oversized loads safely.
•  
4. Late Payment & Storage Delay Penalties
Dead Dingo Dongas is a strict manufacture-to-dispatch business. We do not store finished products on-site. We utilise an outsourced, specialist kit consolidation service that relies on rapid, efficient freight flows.
• Any delays in dispatch caused by late final payments disrupt this freight system.
• Delays due to late payments will incur a $10 per day storage fee.
• This fee will accumulate daily until the outstanding balance is cleared including storage. Only then will the kit  be released for dispatch.
