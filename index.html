What makes it different

Built Different. Built Better.

A standard tin shed is just storage. The Dead Dingo donga is a space you'll actually want to spend time in.

Insulated Walls and Roof – regulates temperature so not only cooler in Summer but warmer in Winter.
  • Simple and efficient for A/C or whirlybird installation
  • Simple for installation of services (electrical, telecommunications, water)
Exempt Development in NSW – saves potentially thousands in DA fees.
  • No waiting months for approvals — just a couple of days to erect with a friend. “How will YOU use your Dead Dingo Donga? You can do more than just store garden tools.”
  • Check council regulations for boundary distances and permissible use.
Full Raised Floor – No concrete required, saves costs, no damage to yard from concrete trucks and avoids access problems.
  • Keeps above the ground during the wet season — means easy installation of drains and other under-floor services.
  • Steel frame with MgO sheeting is oil, water and fire resistant.
Full Head Height – 2.3m internal at wall rising in vaulted roof line.
Full Size Securely Lockable Doors – no stooping getting through the standard door. Standard Door: 970mm W · Double Door: 1,900mm × 2,100mm.
Secure Window – 930mm × 930mm lockable with security grille.
Pest and Fire Resistant – all non-combustible materials.
  • Sealed panel construction with metal on both sides prevents termites or rodents chewing, PLUS prevents moisture ingress.
Easy Construction – full instructions supplied, use standard tools.
Great Sound Reduction – while not fully soundproof, the insulated panel reduces sound considerably.
Transportable – can be relocated in one piece.
